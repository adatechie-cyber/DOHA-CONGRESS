About IPR-CAD
A hybrid, peer-reviewed conference at the intersection of intellectual property, climate justice, and the Doha Declaration paradigm.

Launched in 2019 in the "Covid-style" format of digital posters and 3-minute video presentations. Submissions are archived in Zotero (posters) and Figshare (videos). All outputs receive DOIs. 120 volunteer reviewers from academia and startups apply double-blind evaluation.
